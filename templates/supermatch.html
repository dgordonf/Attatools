SuperMatch

SuperMatch is a tool that allows you to compare two sets of strings and find the best matches.

To use the tool, paste excel columns of strings (words) into each text area below. Then click the "Supermatch" button. The tool will return a table of the best matches and a "Match Score".

The "Match Score" shows how close of a match the app found. A `1` is a perfect match, `0` is a terrible match, `.85` is a decent match. Typically matches around `.85` are good enough, but this will depend on what you are trying to match.
